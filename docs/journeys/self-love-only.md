# Journey: Self-Love Only
**Combination ID:** `self_love`
**Trigger:** User selects only "Self-love" during onboarding Step 1

---

## Who She Is
A woman who came to this app for emotional care, softness, and inner steadiness.
She may be going through a stressful period, recovering from burnout, or simply wanting
a quiet space that nurtures her. She is **not** interested in cycle data or productivity goals.
She wants to feel held, understood, and gently guided toward calm.

---

## Onboarding Flow

| Step | Screen | What She Sees |
|------|--------|--------------|
| 1 | Goals | Selects "Self-love" card only |
| 2 | Privacy Consent | Standard |
| 3 | Health Setup | Light version — general wellness questions, no cycle depth |
| 4 | Emotional Wellness | Full mood wheel + stress slider. Heading: *"How have you been feeling lately?"* Subheading: *"We will tune your Soul space around care, softness, and emotional safety."* |
| 5 | Personalization | Better sleep + stress recovery pre-selected. Evening rhythm pre-selected. Bloop copy: *"Bloop is creating a Soul plan focused on emotional steadiness and softer daily care."* |
| 6 | Ready | Hero message: *"You deserve to feel at home in yourself."* |

---

## App Experience (Post-Onboarding)

### Visible Tabs
```
Dashboard → Wellness → Community → Sleep → Profile
```
> Cycle, Nourish, and Insights tabs are hidden — data-heavy and not her focus.

### Tab Priority Order
1. **Wellness** — breathing sanctuary, her primary destination
2. **Community** — connection, "you are not alone"
3. **Sleep** — rest and recovery as emotional restoration
4. **Dashboard** — affirmation hero, mood check-in
5. **Profile**

### Dashboard Hero
- Warm affirmation card at the top (e.g. *"You showed up for yourself today."*)
- Mood check-in prompt: *"How are you feeling right now?"*
- Bloop greeting tied to emotional state from onboarding
- Today's breathing / grounding suggestion
- No cycle ring, no hormone data, no goal progress

### Bloop Chat Personality
- Tone: deeply empathetic, gentle, non-clinical, like a warm friend
- Opening: *"Hey [name]. I'm here. How are you feeling today?"*
- Focus topics: emotional check-ins, grounding exercises, affirmations, sleep support, community moments
- Proactively surfaces: /grounding route when stress signals are detected

### Wellness Screen
- Opens to Breathing Sanctuary (box breathing) as default
- Phase selector hidden — no cycle phase framing
- Recommendation cards: journaling, evening wind-down, gentle movement

### Community Screen
- Default category: "Emotional Wellness"
- Soli AI card: emotional support framing (not cycle framing)

### Sleep Screen
- Insight: emotional recovery framing — *"Rest is part of healing."*
- Recommended audio: calming rain, soft piano, nature sounds
