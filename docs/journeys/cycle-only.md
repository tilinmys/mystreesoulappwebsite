# Journey: Cycle Only
**Combination ID:** `cycle`
**Trigger:** User selects only "Cycle" during onboarding Step 1

---

## Who She Is
A woman whose primary goal is understanding her body through her menstrual cycle.
She may be tracking for the first time, managing symptoms, or planning around phases.
She does **not** want wellness philosophy, emotional coaching, or goal-setting content.
She wants data, patterns, and practical advice tied directly to where she is in her cycle.

---

## Onboarding Flow

| Step | Screen | What She Sees |
|------|--------|--------------|
| 1 | Goals | Selects "Cycle" card only |
| 2 | Privacy Consent | Standard — no changes |
| 3 | Health Setup | **Cycle-first** — last period date, cycle length, flow, symptoms |
| 4 | Emotional Wellness | Framed as "how does your body feel" not mood check-in. Heading: *"How has your body felt lately?"* |
| 5 | Personalization | Energy + better sleep pre-selected. Bloop copy: *"Bloop is shaping a cycle-aware rhythm with energy, sleep, and symptom timing in mind."* |
| 6 | Ready | Hero message: *"Your cycle is your guide."* |

---

## App Experience (Post-Onboarding)

### Visible Tabs
```
Dashboard → Cycle → Insights → Sleep → Nourish → Profile
```
> Community and Wellness tabs are hidden — not relevant to her stated need.

### Tab Priority Order
1. **Cycle** — her primary destination, shown second (right after Dashboard)
2. **Insights** — hormone timeline, phase predictions
3. **Sleep** — sleep quality is directly tied to cycle phase
4. **Nourish** — phase-specific nutrition
5. **Dashboard** — cycle ring is the hero widget
6. **Profile**

### Dashboard Hero
- Large cycle phase ring front and center
- Current phase label + day count
- "Today for your body" card (cycle-phase specific tip)
- No goal progress bars, no emotional wellness card
- Bloop insight tied to current phase hormone levels

### Bloop Chat Personality
- Tone: informative, body-positive, clinical but warm
- Opening: *"You're on Day X of your cycle — your [phase name] phase. Here's what your body may be experiencing."*
- Focus topics: phase symptoms, hormone levels, energy prediction, sleep quality, nutrition for phase
- Does NOT proactively bring up: emotional coaching, goal streaks, community posts

### Insights Screen
- Hero: Hormone timeline graph (estrogen, progesterone, LH)
- Sections: Phase breakdown, symptom patterns, predicted ovulation, cycle length history
- AI card: *"Based on your last 3 cycles..."*

### Nourish Screen
- Hero: Phase-specific nutrition recommendation
- Filter pre-set to current cycle phase (Menstrual / Follicular / Ovulation / Luteal)
- No generic "wellness goals" framing

### Sleep Screen
- Insight card tied to cycle phase: *"Progesterone drops before your period — this can disrupt your sleep."*
