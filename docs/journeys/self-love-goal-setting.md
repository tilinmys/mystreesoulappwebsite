# Journey: Self-Love + Goal Setting
**Combination ID:** `self_love_goal_setting`
**Trigger:** User selects both "Self-love" AND "Goal setter" during onboarding Step 1

---

## Who She Is
A woman who wants to grow — but gently. She's tired of harsh productivity culture
and wants to build habits from a place of self-compassion, not punishment.
She wants to feel emotionally supported while also making quiet, consistent progress.
She is **not** focused on her cycle — her focus is on emotional health + soft habit-building.

---

## Onboarding Flow

| Step | Screen | What She Sees |
|------|--------|--------------|
| 1 | Goals | Selects "Self-love" + "Goal setter" |
| 2 | Privacy Consent | Standard |
| 3 | Health Setup | Lifestyle + emotional baseline (no cycle depth) |
| 4 | Emotional Wellness | Full mood check-in. Heading: *"What helps you feel cared for and consistent?"* Subheading: *"We will balance emotional support with gentle progress."* |
| 5 | Personalization | Mindfulness + energy pre-selected. Evening rhythm. Bloop copy: *"Bloop is combining emotional reassurance with tiny, repeatable goals."* |
| 6 | Ready | Hero message: *"Progress that feels kind."* |

---

## App Experience (Post-Onboarding)

### Visible Tabs
```
Dashboard → Wellness → Nourish → Community → Sleep → Profile
```
> Cycle and Insights tabs are hidden.

### Tab Priority Order
1. **Wellness** — emotional sanctuary, primary destination
2. **Nourish** — nutrition as self-care (not performance)
3. **Community** — soft accountability through connection
4. **Sleep** — rest as a form of self-investment
5. **Dashboard** — affirmation + gentle goal cards
6. **Profile**

### Dashboard Hero
- Affirmation card at the top — changes daily
- Gentle goal tracker (not a harsh streak counter — framed as *"You've shown up X times this week"*)
- Mood check-in: *"How are you feeling?"*
- Bloop card: emotional + habit combined (*"You've been consistent this week. That takes courage."*)
- Today's suggestion: a small, kind action

### Bloop Chat Personality
- Tone: warm coach meets caring friend — encouraging without pressure
- Opening: *"Hey [name]. How did today feel? I noticed you've been showing up consistently."*
- Focus topics: emotional check-ins, celebrating small wins, gentle habit suggestions, self-compassion prompts, sleep rituals
- Key behaviour: never frames missed habits as failures — always reframes with compassion

### Wellness Screen
- Opens to breathing + affirmation combo
- Recommendation cards: journaling, evening rituals, body scan meditation

### Nourish Screen
- Framed as *"nourishing yourself"* not *"optimising performance"*
- Hero: *"What would feel good to eat today?"*
- No cycle-phase filter shown

### Community Screen
- Default category: *"Emotional Wellness"*
- Soli card: *"Self-compassion is not weakness — it's the foundation of real change."*
