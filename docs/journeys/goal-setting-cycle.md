# Journey: Goal Setting + Cycle
**Combination ID:** `goal_setting_cycle`
**Trigger:** User selects both "Goal setter" AND "Cycle" during onboarding Step 1

---

## Who She Is
A driven, body-aware woman who wants her habits and goals to work *with* her cycle
instead of against it. She may have noticed she's more productive during follicular phase
and exhausted during luteal. She wants to plan smarter — not harder.
Cycle data is a tool for her, not an emotional subject.

---

## Onboarding Flow

| Step | Screen | What She Sees |
|------|--------|--------------|
| 1 | Goals | Selects "Goal setter" + "Cycle" |
| 2 | Privacy Consent | Standard |
| 3 | Health Setup | Full cycle setup + lifestyle/activity baseline |
| 4 | Emotional Wellness | Light framing. Heading: *"What rhythm do you want to build around your cycle?"* Subheading: *"We will pair cycle awareness with practical weekly habits."* |
| 5 | Personalization | Energy + gentle movement pre-selected. Morning rhythm. Bloop copy: *"Bloop is building goals that adapt to cycle timing, energy, and recovery."* |
| 6 | Ready | Hero message: *"Your cycle is your most powerful planning tool."* |

---

## App Experience (Post-Onboarding)

### Visible Tabs
```
Dashboard → Cycle → Nourish → Insights → Sleep → Profile
```
> Wellness and Community tabs are hidden.

### Tab Priority Order
1. **Cycle** — body data drives her planning
2. **Nourish** — phase-optimised nutrition for performance
3. **Insights** — habit data overlaid with cycle phase
4. **Sleep** — recovery planning by phase
5. **Dashboard** — cycle ring + goal progress combined
6. **Profile**

### Dashboard Hero
- Cycle phase ring with an overlay: *"Day 14 · Ovulation phase · High energy window"*
- Goal progress bar below cycle ring (goals tied to phase capacity)
- *"Phase plan for this week"* card — recommended tasks for current phase
- Quick log: cycle symptoms + habit completion

### Bloop Chat Personality
- Tone: strategic, practical, body-data informed, energising
- Opening: *"You're in your [phase] phase — your energy is [high/moderate/low] right now. Want to see this week's plan?"*
- Focus topics: energy mapping by phase, optimal exercise timing, nutrition for phase, habit planning, recovery days
- Key behaviour: proactively suggests *lighter* goals during luteal/menstrual phase

### Insights Screen
- Hero: Habit completion rate overlaid with cycle phase
- *"You complete 78% of your goals during follicular phase vs 42% during luteal."*
- Phase performance breakdown
- Energy + cycle correlation graph

### Nourish Screen
- Phase filter always synced to current cycle phase
- *"Luteal phase — your body needs magnesium and complex carbs right now."*
- Meal cards: phase-optimised recipes

### Sleep Screen
- *"Progesterone peaks in your luteal phase — prioritise 8 hours this week."*
