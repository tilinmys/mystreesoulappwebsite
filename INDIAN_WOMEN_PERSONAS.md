# MyStree Soul — Indian Women Personas Database (Ages 15-35)

This database contains 15 diverse and realistic personas representing Indian women in the 15-35 age range. It is structured to help test UI/UX compatibility, track features, and identify user pain points across our three core active onboarding pathways: Adolescent/Teen Care, Cycle tracking & Fertility, and Pregnancy.
