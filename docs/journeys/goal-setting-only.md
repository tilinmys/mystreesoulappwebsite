# Journey: Goal Setting Only
**Combination ID:** `goal_setting`
**Trigger:** User selects only "Goal setter" during onboarding Step 1

---

## Who She Is
A woman who wants structure, accountability, and habit-building.
She came to build a healthier routine — better nutrition, more movement, consistent sleep.
She is driven, practical, and wants to see progress. She is **not** here for cycle tracking
or emotional deep-dives. She wants a clear plan and a sense of forward momentum.

---

## Onboarding Flow

| Step | Screen | What She Sees |
|------|--------|--------------|
| 1 | Goals | Selects "Goal setter" card only |
| 2 | Privacy Consent | Standard |
| 3 | Health Setup | Focus on lifestyle — sleep schedule, activity level, nutrition habits |
| 4 | Emotional Wellness | Framed as baseline check-in. Heading: *"What kind of support keeps you steady?"* Subheading: *"We will shape your plan around small goals and repeatable habits."* |
| 5 | Personalization | Energy + hydration pre-selected. Morning rhythm pre-selected. Bloop copy: *"Bloop is building a simple plan around small wins, timing, and habit consistency."* |
| 6 | Ready | Hero message: *"Small steps. Real change."* |

---

## App Experience (Post-Onboarding)

### Visible Tabs
```
Dashboard → Nourish → Insights → Sleep → Profile
```
> Cycle, Wellness, and Community tabs are hidden.

### Tab Priority Order
1. **Nourish** — nutrition habits, her primary destination
2. **Insights** — habit streak graphs, progress over time
3. **Sleep** — sleep as a productivity and recovery tool
4. **Dashboard** — goal cards, streak counters, today's plan
5. **Profile**

### Dashboard Hero
- Daily goal progress bar front and center
- Habit streak counter (consecutive days logged)
- *"Today's plan"* card with 3 actionable items
- Quick log: water, movement, meals
- No cycle ring, no emotional wellness card, no phase labels

### Bloop Chat Personality
- Tone: supportive but focused, like a wellness coach
- Opening: *"Morning [name]. You've logged X days in a row. Here's what I'd focus on today."*
- Focus topics: habit tracking, nutrition advice, sleep quality, hydration, movement suggestions
- Does NOT proactively bring up: emotional coaching, cycle symptoms, community posts

### Insights Screen
- Hero: Habit consistency graph (7-day, 30-day view)
- Sections: hydration trends, sleep quality, nutrition score, movement frequency
- No hormone data, no cycle phase labels
- AI card: *"Your energy is highest on days you hit 7+ hours sleep."*

### Nourish Screen
- Hero: Today's nutrition focus (general — not cycle-phase specific)
- Phase filter hidden
- Quick ideas: meal prep, protein-rich choices, hydration reminders

### Sleep Screen
- Insight: productivity framing — *"7-9 hours tonight will directly impact tomorrow's energy and focus."*
