# Journey: Self-Love + Cycle
**Combination ID:** `self_love_cycle`
**Trigger:** User selects both "Self-love" AND "Cycle" during onboarding Step 1

---

## Who She Is
A woman who wants to understand both her body and her emotions — and crucially,
how the two connect. She may be experiencing mood swings tied to her cycle,
anxiety before her period, or emotional exhaustion she suspects is hormonal.
She wants cycle data AND emotional safety in the same space.

---

## Onboarding Flow

| Step | Screen | What She Sees |
|------|--------|--------------|
| 1 | Goals | Selects "Self-love" + "Cycle" |
| 2 | Privacy Consent | Standard |
| 3 | Health Setup | Full cycle setup + mood/emotional baseline questions |
| 4 | Emotional Wellness | Heading: *"How are your emotions moving with your body?"* Subheading: *"We will connect mood, cycle, rest, and reassurance."* |
| 5 | Personalization | Stress recovery + better sleep pre-selected. Evening rhythm. Bloop copy: *"Bloop is connecting mood, rest, and cycle patterns into a softer daily rhythm."* |
| 6 | Ready | Hero message: *"Your feelings and your cycle tell the same story."* |

---

## App Experience (Post-Onboarding)

### Visible Tabs
```
Dashboard → Cycle → Wellness → Community → Sleep → Profile
```
> Nourish and Insights tabs are secondary — accessible but not front-and-centre.

### Tab Priority Order
1. **Cycle** — body awareness first
2. **Wellness** — emotional care second
3. **Community** — she wants to know others feel this too
4. **Sleep** — rest and emotional recovery
5. **Dashboard** — dual-focus: cycle ring + mood check-in
6. **Profile**

### Dashboard Hero
- Split hero: Cycle phase ring (left) + Mood check-in (right)
- *"Your [phase] phase may be affecting how you feel today."* — the connection message
- Bloop greeting: ties current cycle phase to emotional state
- Today's card: emotional + physical support combined (e.g. *"Progesterone is peaking — this is a great day for gentle movement and self-care."*)

### Bloop Chat Personality
- Tone: warm, validating, connects physical and emotional dots
- Opening: *"You're in your [phase] phase, [name]. How are you feeling emotionally today?"*
- Focus topics: mood-cycle connection, PMS emotional patterns, reassurance, sleep and cycle, grounding exercises
- Key behaviour: when she logs a mood, Bloop cross-references it with her cycle phase

### Insights Screen (When Accessed)
- Mood + hormone overlay graph (show both on same timeline)
- *"Your anxiety spikes in your luteal phase"* type insights
- Phase breakdown includes emotional trends

### Wellness Screen
- Phase-aware recommendations (e.g. *"In your luteal phase, try gentler breathing."*)
- Phase selector shown and synced to current cycle phase

### Community Screen
- Default category: *"Emotional Wellness"* or *"PMS & Mood"*
- Stories surfaced: mood + cycle combination themes
