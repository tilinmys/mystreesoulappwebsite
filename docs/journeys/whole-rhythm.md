# Journey: Whole Rhythm (All Three)
**Combination ID:** `whole_rhythm`
**Trigger:** User selects "Self-love" + "Goal setter" + "Cycle" during onboarding Step 1

---

## Who She Is
A woman who wants everything — emotional care, body awareness, and purposeful habits.
She is open to the full app experience and wants Bloop to weave all three threads together.
She may be just starting her wellness journey and wants to explore, or she's experienced
and wants a unified space that honours her whole self.

## App Experience (Post-Onboarding)

### Visible Tabs
```
Dashboard → Cycle → Wellness → Nourish → Insights → Sleep → Community → Profile
```
> All tabs visible. Full app experience.

### Tab Priority Order
Balanced — no single tab dominates. Dashboard surfaces all three pillars equally.

### Dashboard Hero
- **Three-pillar card layout:**
  1. Cycle phase ring + today's phase label
  2. Mood / emotional check-in
  3. Today's goal progress
- Bloop greeting: weaves all three (*"You're in [phase], feeling [mood], and you've completed [X] of today's goals."*)
- *"Today's rhythm"* card: suggests one action from each pillar

### Bloop Chat Personality
- Tone: holistic, integrated, like a wise personal wellness guide
- Opening: *"[name], let's look at your whole rhythm today — your body, your emotions, and your goals."*
- Focus topics: all three pillars, cross-references between them
- Key behaviour: surfaces connections (*"Your stress is higher this week — it's your luteal phase AND you've been pushing hard on your goals. Let's find balance."*)

### All Tabs: Full Experience
- Cycle screen: full phase ring, hormone graph, daily log
- Wellness: phase-aware breathing, emotional support
- Nourish: cycle-synced nutrition + habit support
- Insights: all data layers — mood + hormone + habit overlay
- Sleep: cycle + emotional + recovery synthesis
- Community: all categories accessible

## Bloop's Unique Behaviour for Whole Rhythm
Bloop actively looks for **cross-pillar patterns** and surfaces them:
- *"Every time you're in your luteal phase, your goal completion drops. Want to try lighter goals this week?"*
- *"Your sleep improved the weeks you did 3+ breathing sessions. I'll remind you tonight."*
- *"You haven't logged your mood in 3 days — that usually means you're overwhelmed. Want to talk?"*

---

## Key Metric for This Journey
> **Cross-pillar engagement** — is she using at least 2 of the 3 pillars each week?
